## Project 1: High-Value Active Customers

### Business Objective
Identify customers with lifetime spend greater than $1,000 who have also made a purchase within the last 100 days.

### Approach
- Aggregated lifetime spend per customer
- Identified recent purchasers (last 100 days)
- Joined both datasets using CTEs to isolate high-value active customers

See revenue_segmentation_analysis.sql for the full query.
